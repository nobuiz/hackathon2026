ReferralGuard

A multi-agent pre-flight check for specialty referrals & prior authorizations — catching denials before submission, with a watchable, timestamped audit trail.
The pitch: ReferralGuard reads an incoming referral or prior-auth request, extracts the key fields, flags everything missing or likely to be denied — each with a stated reason — and shows a replayable audit trail of exactly what each agent checked and why. It turns reactive, after-the-fact denials into a fix made before the request is ever sent.

The solution — 3 steps, live

1
Extract patient, ICD-10 diagnosis, CPT procedure, and insurance from messy fax / EHR / phone-voice intake.
2
Flag what's missing or likely to be denied — undocumented step therapy, unreadable member ID — each with a written reason.
3
Show the audit trail — a timestamped, replayable trace of every agent decision. Watchable in under 30 seconds.

Why this could be a company

Every denied or returned prior auth is lost clinic revenue and a delayed patient. ReferralGuard sits at the point of submission and converts after-the-fact denials into pre-submission fixes, with an audit trail payers and compliance teams can trust — a per-seat SaaS wedge into a workflow every specialist hits ~40 times a week.
